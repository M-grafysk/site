About Allwood

Allwood is a range of luxury wood finishes from Grasim Industries Ltd (Birla Paints Div.) which is part of the Aditya Birla Group, a global conglomerate, in the league of Fortune 500 companies. The Allwood range of wood finishes incorporates the latest Italian technology and would strive to bring consumers the highest standards of quality and finesse.

Our products have been formulated at ALCEA, Italy keeping in mind the latest trends in the Indian Wood Coatings market. A home is a symbol of aspirations, and our intention is to enhance it with durable coatings that withstand the passage of time.

Our vision is to offer real guidance and support to consumers so that they can choose the best wood coating solutions for their dream home

About ABG

Entering paints business

The Aditya Birla Group is entering the paints business through a new venture announced by flagship company Grasim Industries Ltd. The company plans to introduce the latest range of paint products in line with global mega-trends.

The entry of Grasim in the paints sector will offer a wide choice to Indian consumers as the Company plans to introduce the latest range of paint products in line with global mega-trends. The Company’s entry into this high growth sector will help painters/ applicators and all traditional and emerging channel partners across India to expand their existing business and grow.
